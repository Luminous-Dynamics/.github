## Expected Behavior

We expect all community members to:

- **Practice kindness and empathy** in all interactions
- **Assume positive intent** when engaging with others
- **Welcome diversity** of thought, background, and experience
- **Give and receive feedback gracefully** with constructive intent
- **Respect boundaries** - both technical and personal
- **Acknowledge contributions** and practice gratitude
- **Act with integrity** aligned with our stated principles
- **Support newcomers** with patience and generosity

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not aligned with our community values:

- **Harassment or discrimination** of any kind
- **Personal attacks** or ad hominem arguments
- **Trolling, insulting comments**, or sustained disruption
- **Doxxing or privacy violations** - sharing private information
- **Unwelcome sexual attention** or advances
- **Advocating for or encouraging** any of the above behaviors
- **Exploitation** - treating people as mere resources
- **Bad faith engagement** - intentional derailing or disruption

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All Luminous Dynamics repositories
- GitHub discussions, issues, and pull requests
- Community communication channels
- Events and gatherings (virtual or in-person)
- Any space where you're representing Luminous Dynamics

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

1. **For immediate safety concerns**: Remove yourself from the situation
2. **Report to**: invest@luminousdynamics.org
3. **Provide details**: What happened, when, where, who was involved
4. **Confidentiality**: Reports will be handled with discretion

### Response

Community maintainers will:

1. **Acknowledge receipt** of the report within 48 hours
2. **Investigate thoroughly** with care and confidentiality
3. **Respond appropriately** based on severity and context
4. **Support affected parties** throughout the process

### Consequences

Depending on the violation, responses may include:

- **Private conversation** to clarify expectations
- **Public warning** for minor or first-time violations
- **Temporary suspension** from community spaces
- **Permanent ban** for severe or repeated violations

## Restorative Justice

Where appropriate, we favor **restorative approaches**:

- Understanding the harm caused
- Taking responsibility
- Making amends and repairing relationships
- Learning and growth for all involved

We believe in the possibility of transformation and redemption.
