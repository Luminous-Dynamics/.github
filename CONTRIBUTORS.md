## üèÜ Contribution Levels

We recognize different levels of engagement:

### üå± Contributor
**First contribution**: Anyone who has made their first contribution

**Recognition**:
- Listed in CONTRIBUTORS.md
- Mentioned in release notes
- Welcome in community discussions

### üåø Regular Contributor
**5+ merged contributions** OR **significant impact** from fewer contributions

**Recognition**:
- Featured in project README
- Invited to contributor discussions
- Priority support from maintainers
- Listed as team member (if desired)

### üå≥ Core Contributor
**25+ merged contributions** OR **sustained engagement** over 6+ months

**Recognition**:
- Triage and label permissions
- Voice in technical decisions
- Listed in organization profile
- Invited to strategic planning

### üåü Maintainer
**Trusted with project stewardship**, typically after 50+ contributions and 12+ months

**Recognition**:
- Commit access to repositories
- Authority over project direction
- Mentorship responsibilities
- Listed as project maintainer

### üíé Steward
**Organization-level leadership**, guiding Luminous Dynamics mission

**Recognition**:
- Organization admin access
- Strategic decision-making
- Community governance role
- Ambassadorship opportunities

## üôè How to Get Recognized

### For Contributors
1. **Make contributions**: Submit PRs, open issues, participate in discussions
2. **Document your work**: Clear PR descriptions help us recognize your impact
3. **Update your profile**: Let us know if you'd like to be listed as a team member
4. **Nominate others**: Recognize fellow contributors in Discussions

### For Maintainers
1. **Use All-Contributors bot**: Add contributors to project READMEs
2. **Mention in release notes**: Credit contributors in changelogs
3. **Promote when appropriate**: Suggest elevation to higher contribution levels
4. **Celebrate publicly**: Share achievements in Discussions and social media

## üìù Self-Nomination

Don't wait to be noticed! Self-nomination is encouraged:

1. Open a Discussion with tag `[Recognition]`
2. Share your contributions and impact
3. Request the level of recognition you're seeking
4. Maintainers will review and respond

We honor self-advocacy as an act of clarity, not ego.

## üåä Contribution Guidelines

When contributing, consider:

- **Alignment with ERC**: Does this serve consciousness-first principles?
- **Community benefit**: How does this help all beings?
- **Quality and care**: Is this your best sacred offering?
- **Collaboration**: Have you engaged with the community?
- **Documentation**: Have you explained your contribution clearly?
