# Recommended Pinned Repositories

This document provides recommendations for which repositories to pin on the Luminous Dynamics organization profile page.

## Why Pin Repositories?

Pinned repositories appear prominently on your organization's GitHub page and should showcase:
- Your most important/flagship projects
- Active, well-maintained repositories
- Projects that best represent your mission
- A diverse representation of your work
- Live/production systems when available

## How to Pin Repositories

1. Go to https://github.com/Luminous-Dynamics
2. Click "Customize your pins" below the pinned repositories section
3. Select up to 6 repositories to feature
4. Drag to reorder (top-left position is most prominent)
5. Save changes
